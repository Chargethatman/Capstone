Food Banks and Pantries

  • Durham Community Food Pantry - Distributes food on Wednesdays (10:00 am-1:00 pm and 5:00 pm-7:00 pm) and Thursdays (10:00 am-1:00 pm). Case management services are available by appointment.
  • Walltown Neighborhood Ministries Food Bank - Operates through Asbury United Methodist Church in Durham, NC 27705. Contact them directly for dates and hours of food distribution.
  • Mt Calvary United Church of Christ - Offers food assistance. You can call (919) 688-5066 for more information.
  • Two Fish - Five Loaves - Operates on the 2nd and 4th Thursday of each month from 4:00 pm - 7:00 pm in Durham.
  • The Refuge House - Provides food distribution on the 3rd or 4th Thursday of each month from 3:00 pm - 5:00 pm.
  • Calvary Baptist Church - Distributes food on the 3rd Thursday of the month from 6:30 pm - 7:30 pm; clients must call earlier in the day to sign up.
  • For a comprehensive list of all food assistance locations in Durham, you can visit FoodPantries.org.

Housing Resources

  • Durham Housing Authority (DHA) - The main public housing authority providing affordable housing in Durham. For more information on services and programs, visit the Durham Housing Authority website or contact them directly.
  • Urban Ministries of Durham - Provides shelter and supportive services for the homeless. For more details, visit Urban Ministries of Durham.

Other Resources

  • Durham Technical Community College - Provides education and job training opportunities.
  • Lincoln Community Health Center - Offers medical care and mental health services for low-income individuals.
  • Durham Crisis Response Center - Provides support for individuals facing domestic violence and sexual assault.
  • CAARE Inc. - Offers a variety of services including health care, job placement, and legal assistance.
