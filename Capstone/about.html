About Compassion Compass

Compassion Compass is a project dedicated to assisting the unsheltered population in locating essential resources efficiently. Our goal is to connect individuals in need with local support services in the Downtown Durham and NCCU areas through innovative technology solutions.

Project Vision

Compassion Compass aims to provide a geolocation application that assists the unsheltered with information and connection to local resources, including shelters, food, clothing, and other assistance services. Our interactive maps and feedback mechanisms will help non-profit organizations better serve those in need.
